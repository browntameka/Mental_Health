What is Mental Health?

Mental health includes emotional, psychological, and social well-being.

According WHO (2025)it is a state of mental well-being that enables people to cope with the stresses of life, realizing their abilities, learn and work productive, so that they can contribute to their community.It exist on a complex continuum, where no one person have the same experience at any time.
